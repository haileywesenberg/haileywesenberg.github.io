Where to Shop in Ventura

Ventura is full of unique boutiques, surf-inspired brands, thrift gems, and locally made jewelry. Whether you're looking for coastal style or vintage treasures, these spots are worth visiting.

Clothing Stores

  • Rip Curl: Coastal surf style and beachwear.
  • Findings Flea: Curated vintage and trendy pieces.
  • Patagonia: Sustainable outdoor apparel.

Thrift Stores

  • Coalition Thrift: Affordable finds supporting community causes.
  • Avenue Thrift: Hidden treasures and vintage gems.
  • The Bargain Box: Budget-friendly and community focused.

Jewelry

  • Betty Belts: Handmade coastal-inspired accessories.
  • Daisy Chain Permanent Jewelry: Trendy custom-fit pieces.
