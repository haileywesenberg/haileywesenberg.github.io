Outdoor Activities in Ventura

From ocean views to scenic hikes and sunset lookouts, Ventura offers endless ways to enjoy the outdoors. Whether you're relaxing by the water or exploring local trails, there are views all around.

Beaches

  • Solimar Beach Quiet shoreline with beautiful sunsets.
  • Surfer's Point Popular surf and ocean views.
  • Pierpont Beach Relaxed local beach community.

Hikes

  • Ventura Botanical Gardens Scenic hillside gardens overlooking the ocean.
  • Arroyo Verde Park Family-friendly trails with city views.
  • Harmon Canyon Local secluded hiking area.

Lookouts

  • Serra Cross Park Classic Ventura overlook at sunset.
  • Ventura Pier The oldest California pier looking out over the Pacific Ocean.

You can check current beach conditions on the City of Ventura website .
